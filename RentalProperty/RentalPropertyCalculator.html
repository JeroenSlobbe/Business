Rental Property Performance Evaluator

The goal of this rental property performance evaluator is to evaluate the financial performance of a property being rented to one or multiple tenants. The calculator does not factor in profits or losses that come from changes in the value of the underlying property, nor does it factor in depreciation of the assets. The tool is built to quickly assess the operating performance of the rental asset.
